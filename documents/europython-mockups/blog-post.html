Become a Codemotion Speaker

The Codemotion program will be defined by a public Call for Papers open to all. Proposals will be selected by representatives of official Italian communities for each different language as well as for software development. Have you got an interesting idea for a talk? www.codemotion.it

The Codemotion program will be defined by a public Call for Papers open to all. Proposals will be selected by representatives of official Italian communities for each different language as well as for software development.

Participants looking for new career opportunities may submit their CVs to Codemotion sponsors at the event. A fast-paced morning of technical talks followed by two sessions of Ignite in the afternoon are on the day’s program. A competition to award the best in innovative ideas developed by young university students from all over Italy is currently in the works.

Have you got an interesting idea for a talk?

Read the guidelines and submit your proposal! Representatives of official Italian communities related to the languages and software development of Codemotion will be in charge of the selection process. The deadline is 18 January 2011 (11:59pm).

Codemotion will be held in Rome on 5 March 2011. As always, this is a free event. For information: www.codemotion.it
